# GSTD Platform Manifesto
## The Future of Decentralized Physical Infrastructure (DePIN)

### The Vision

In an era where centralized cloud computing giants control access to computational resources, the GSTD Platform emerges as a revolutionary force—democratizing access to distributed computing through blockchain technology and gold-backed stability.

### Why GSTD + XAUt?

**Decentralization Meets Stability**

Traditional DePIN projects face a critical challenge: token volatility undermines economic incentives. Workers hesitate to contribute resources when rewards can lose value overnight. Requesters struggle to budget when costs fluctuate unpredictably.

GSTD solves this by backing platform fees with **XAUt (Tether Gold)**—real gold on the blockchain. Every 5% platform fee is automatically converted to XAUt, creating a growing Golden Reserve that:

- **Provides Stability**: Gold-backed reserves ensure long-term value preservation
- **Builds Trust**: Transparent, on-chain treasury visible to all participants
- **Incentivizes Participation**: Workers know their ecosystem is backed by real assets
- **Enables Growth**: Stable foundation allows for sustainable network expansion

### The 95/5 Economic Model

**Fair Distribution, Sustainable Growth**

- **95% to Workers**: The vast majority of task budgets go directly to computing nodes—rewarding those who power the network
- **5% to Platform**: A minimal fee that funds operations and builds the Golden Reserve

This model ensures:
- Workers are fairly compensated for their computational contributions
- The platform remains sustainable without excessive fees
- The Golden Reserve grows organically with network activity
- Long-term value accrual benefits all participants
